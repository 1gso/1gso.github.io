Opus Magnum
Four transmutations, one philosopher's stone
⟐ Hyperworker — The Observer ⟐
Regime-aware overseer — observation alters the system
[ tap to reveal ]
The Hyperworker sits above all four strategies as a regime-detection engine. It fuses two signal classes:

Micro detectors — news flow, order flow, sentiment, positioning data — the fast-twitch fibres that sense shifts before they show in price.

Macro flow — rates, yields, cross-asset momentum, liquidity conditions — the slow structural currents that define the prevailing regime.

Together they form a detecting machine for regime changes. The Hyperworker's observations propagate downward — modulating position sizing, strategy weighting, and risk budgets across all four books. The act of observation itself alters the system.
I
Stat Arb / Pairs
Explaining & exploiting capital flows
[ tap ]
Pairs trading and statistical arbitrage grounded in the mechanics of capital flows — not mere correlation. The first-level magic word is the why behind co-movement: sector rotation, index rebalancing, fund flows, and liquidity-driven dislocations. Cointegration is the symptom; capital flow is the cause.
II
Factor Investing
Embracing complexity
[ tap ]
Beyond the canonical factor zoo — embracing complexity means accepting that factor premia are nonlinear, time-varying, and interact in ways linear models cannot capture. Covariance denoising (RMT / Marchenko-Pastur), robust PCA, and deep factor models replace the classical Fama-French decomposition. Complexity is not the enemy; oversimplification is.
III
Trend Following
Change point dynamics
[ tap ]
Classical trend following reimagined through change point detection. Rather than fixed lookback windows and moving average crossovers, the magic word is identifying structural breaks — inflection points where the generating process shifts. Bayesian online change point detection, Hurst exponent estimation, and hidden Markov models replace the blunt instruments of CTA lore.
IV
Macro
Conditional portfolio optimization
[ tap ]
Macro allocation driven by conditional portfolio optimization — expected returns, covariances, and constraints are all regime-dependent. The optimization surface reshapes itself based on the Hyperworker's regime classification. No single efficient frontier; rather a manifold of frontiers indexed by the prevailing macro state.
